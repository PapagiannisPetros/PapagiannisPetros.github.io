Covers GPS spoofing, jamming, data-link interception, malware injection, and command hijacking, along with countermeasures such as cryptographic protocols, intrusion detection, and resilient control architectures.

Covers reconnaissance, vulnerability scanning, exploitation, post-exploitation, and privilege escalation, providing hands-on experience with real-world attack vectors in controlled lab conditions.
